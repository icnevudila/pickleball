# 00 — Executive Brief

## Product name

PadelOS

## One-liner

The live operating system for modern padel clubs.

## What it does

PadelOS helps a padel club manage everything that happens before, during and after a match:

- Public booking link for players
- Club/admin app for staff
- Live court board for real-time operations
- Queue and waiting list automation
- Open match creation and player matching
- Payments, deposits and cancellation control
- Player profiles, club leaderboard and challenges

## Product principles

1. **The court is the center.** Every screen should make court status instantly readable.
2. **Booking must feel effortless.** Public booking should not feel like admin software.
3. **Queue is not a spreadsheet.** It is a live operational layer with clear next actions.
4. **Community brings retention.** Player profile, leaderboard and challenges create a reason to return.
5. **Staff need speed, not decoration.** Admin screens should prioritize action clarity.

## Primary audiences

### Club owner

Wants higher utilization, fewer manual messages, fewer empty slots, clearer revenue and a modern brand experience.

### Front desk / staff

Wants to see what is happening now, call the next queue member, change a booking, mark payment, start/end matches and handle exceptions fast.

### Player

Wants to book quickly, find partners, join open matches, track activity and see ranking/challenges.

## Positioning

PadelOS sits between booking platforms and social sport apps:

- Playtomic/MATCHi style booking clarity
- Strava-style activity and competition layer
- Resy/OpenTable-style queue logic
- Square/Mindbody-style business operations

## MVP goal

Launch a club-ready beta that can:

- Publish a public booking page
- Manage court schedules
- Show live court state
- Handle a basic queue
- Store reservations and players
- Accept or record payments
- Display a lightweight player community layer
