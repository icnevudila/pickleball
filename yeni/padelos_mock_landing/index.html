Queue engine

When a court opens, the system already knows who to call.

Queue should not be a receptionist’s memory test. PadelOS turns waitlist, no-show risk and next available court into a staff-ready workflow.

Waitlist becomes revenue recovery.

Short cancellations, rain changes, late finishes and open-match gaps are routed to the right players before the slot goes cold.
